Examples of web design I'm into:

  • mylapka.com- with a beautifully minimal full-screen design, this site grabs my interest even though I have no idea what they're selling.
  • subtlepatterns.com- this site is easy to navigate and provides all the tools you need to utilize their lovely background patterns within each post.
  • mattroeser.com- a clean, simple site that shows off the designer's beautiful book covers without getting in the way.
  • builtbybuffalo.com- by utilizing uncommon shapes (octogons, circles) as a base for their design, and integrating infographics throughout the page, this site really stands out.
  • thisissand.com- a really fun, interactive flash site that allows you to create art with virtual sand.

Why am I taking this class?

I've always had a strong interest in web design as part of my overarching interest in all thing GD. Since the summer web design class I took in 5th grade, I've enjoyed a limited (but gradually expanding) knowledge of html and css. Rarely has my knowledge been put to use outside of customizing tumblr themes, and I'd like to gain the skills I need to really build a site from the ground up. There's so much I don't yet know, especially in the realm of javascript, and I'm really excited to learn.
