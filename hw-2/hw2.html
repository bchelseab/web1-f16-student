Spaghetti Summit 2016

November 18-20 | Boston, MA

A gathering of the world's most passionate pasta manufacturers, chefs, and hobbyists.

This year's highly anticipated summit of all things pasta features demonstrations, panels, and exhibitions of the latest in technology and technique. Culminating in a spaghetti dinner to raise money for the Boston Food Pantry, this weekend of learning and eating is a must-attend for both industry experts and amatuer cooks.
