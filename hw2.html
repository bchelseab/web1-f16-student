Spaghetti Summit 2016

November 18-20 • Boston, MA

A gathering of the world's most passionate pasta manufacturers, chefs, and hobbyists.

Where When How
Hynes Convention center
900 Boylston St, Boston, MA 02115		 Friday 6pm-10pm, Saturday 10am-10pm, Sunday 12pm-9pm Register by 10/29/16 at 6pm EST
This year's highly anticipated summit of all things pasta will feature demonstrations, panels, and exhibitions of the latest in technology and technique. Culminating in a spaghetti dinner to raise money for the Boston Food Pantry, this weekend of learning and eating is a must-attend for both industry experts and amatuer cooks.
